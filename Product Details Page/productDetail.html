boAt Storm

The boAt Watch Storm is the perfect companion for your fitness transformation. Your Watch Storm is here to remove all obstructions that you may face in your fitness journey. With its daily activity tracker and 9 different sports modes, it will now be fun to track your journey towards health. Stay alert at all times with smart notifications from your phone with vibration alerts for calls, texts, schedule reminders, alarms, & more! The “wellness mode” of the smartwatch monitors your sleep, heart rate, blood oxygen levels as well as guides you into a meditative state when you need to relax.
